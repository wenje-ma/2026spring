## 5.4

### 1

利用定理 5.3 (在$\boldsymbol{y}$为列向量的情况) 证明

$$
\boldsymbol{z}=\frac{\partial \boldsymbol{\varphi}}{\partial \lambda}(\boldsymbol{x}, \lambda)=\left(\frac{\partial \varphi_i}{\partial \lambda_k}(\boldsymbol{x}, \lambda)\right)_{n \times m}
$$
满足线性（变分）方程

$$
\frac{d \boldsymbol{z}}{d \boldsymbol{x}}=\boldsymbol{A}(\boldsymbol{x}, \lambda) \boldsymbol{z}+\boldsymbol{B}(\boldsymbol{x}, \lambda)
$$

和初值条件$\boldsymbol{z}(\boldsymbol{x}_0, \lambda)=\boldsymbol{0}$，其中

$$
\boldsymbol{A}(\boldsymbol{x}, \lambda)=\boldsymbol{f}_\boldsymbol{y}'(\boldsymbol{x}, \boldsymbol{\varphi}(\boldsymbol{x}, \lambda), \lambda)=\left(\frac{\partial f_i}{\partial y_j}(\boldsymbol{x}, \boldsymbol{\varphi}(\boldsymbol{x}, \lambda), \lambda)\right)_{n \times n}
$$

和

$$
\boldsymbol{B}(\boldsymbol{x}, \lambda)=\boldsymbol{f}_\lambda'(\boldsymbol{x}, \boldsymbol{\varphi}(\boldsymbol{x}, \lambda), \lambda)=\left(\frac{\partial f_i}{\partial \lambda_k}(\boldsymbol{x}, \boldsymbol{\varphi}(\boldsymbol{x}, \lambda), \lambda)\right)_{n \times m}
$$

试在$\boldsymbol{y}$为行向量的情况考虑相应的变分方程。

#### 解答

已知原方程为

$\begin{cases} \frac{d\varphi}{dx} = f(x, \varphi, \lambda) \\ \varphi(x_0, \lambda) = \varphi_0 \end{cases}$，

两侧对$\lambda_k$求偏导，交换求导顺序：

$\frac{d}{dx}\left( \frac{\partial \varphi}{\partial \lambda_k} \right) = \frac{\partial f}{\partial y} \cdot \frac{\partial \varphi}{\partial \lambda_k} + \frac{\partial f}{\partial \lambda_k}$，

这对应$\frac{dz}{dx} = A(x,\lambda)z + B(x,\lambda)$。

由于$\varphi(x_0, \lambda) = \varphi_0$，

因此$z(x_0, \lambda) = \frac{\partial \varphi}{\partial \lambda}(x_0, \lambda) = 0$。

### 2

在本节最后的推论中，试求$\boldsymbol{z}=\frac{\partial \boldsymbol{\varphi}}{\partial \eta}(\boldsymbol{x}, \eta)$所满足的微分方程和初值条件。（只要求作形式的计算）

#### 解答

由推论知：

对初值问题

$\begin{cases} \frac{d\varphi(x,\eta)}{dx} = f(x, \varphi(x,\eta)) \\ \varphi(x,\eta) = \eta \end{cases}$，

其解$y = \varphi(x, \eta)$在区域$D: |x-x_0| \le \frac{b}{2}, |y-\eta| \le \frac{b}{2}$上连续可微。

对于$\frac{d\varphi(x, \eta)}{dx} = f(x, \varphi(x, \eta))$，

两侧对$\eta$求偏导，交换求导顺序：

$\frac{d}{dx}\left( \frac{\partial \varphi}{\partial \eta} \right) = \frac{\partial f}{\partial y}(x, \varphi) \cdot \frac{\partial \varphi}{\partial \eta}$，

因此$\frac{dz}{dx} = f_y'(x, \varphi) \cdot z$。

对$\varphi(x_0, \eta) = \eta$两侧对$\eta$求偏导：

$\frac{\partial \varphi}{\partial \eta}(x_0, \eta) = 1$，

即$z(x_0, \eta) = 1$。
