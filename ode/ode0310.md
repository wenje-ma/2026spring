**1**

求解微分方程:

(2)

$\frac{dy}{dx}+y\tan x=\sin2x$;

(4)

$\frac{dy}{dx}-\frac{1}{1-x^2}y=1+x,\quad y(0)=1$.

**解答**

(2)

这是一阶线性非齐次方程,故由通解公式:

$y=e^{-\int\tan xdx}(C+\int\sin2xe^{\int\tan xdx}dx)$

$=\cos x(C-2\cos x)=-2\cos ^2x+C\cos x$.

(4)

这是一阶线性非齐次方程,故由通解公式:

$y=e^{\int\frac{1}{1-x^2}dx}(C+\int(x+1)e^{-\int\frac{1}{1-x^2}dx}dx)$

$=\sqrt{\frac{1+x}{1-x}}(C+\int\sqrt{1-x^2}dx)$

$=\sqrt{\frac{1+x}{1-x}}(C+\frac{x}{2}\sqrt{1-x^2}+\frac{1}{2}\arcsin x)$.

由于$y(0)=1$,则$C=1$,即

$y=\sqrt{\frac{1+x}{1-x}}(\frac{x}{2}\sqrt{1-x^2}+\frac{1}{2}\arcsin x+1)$.

**3**

设$y=\varphi(x)$满足微分不等式

$y'+a(x)y\le0\quad(x\ge0)$,

求证:

$\varphi(x)\le\varphi(0){e}^{-\int_0^xa(s)ds}\quad(x\ge0)$.

**解答**

已知$y'+a(x)y=0$是一阶线性齐次方程,

则由通解公式:$y=Ce^{-\int a(x)dx}$是其通解.

令$f(x)=\varphi(x)e^{\int_0^xa(s)ds}$,

则$f'(x)=e^{\int_0^xa(s)ds}(\varphi(x)+a(x)\varphi(x))$,因此$f'(x)\le 0$.

由于$f(0)=\varphi(0)$,则$f(x)\le f(0)$,证毕.

**4**

用常数变易法求解非齐次线性方程

$\frac{dy}{dx}+p(x)y=q(x)$,

即假设该方程有形如$y=Ce^{-\int p(x)dx}$的解,但其中的常数$C$“变易”为$x$的一个待定函数$C(x)$;然后将这种形式的解代入方程,再去确定$C(x)$.

**解答**

将齐次方程的解代入非齐次方程中:

$\frac{dy}{dx}+p(x)C(x)e^{-\int p(x)dx}=q(x)$.

由于$y=C(x)e^{-\int p(x)dx}$,

则$\frac{dy}{dx}=C'(x)e^{-\int p(x)dx}-C(x)p(x)e^{-\int p(x)dx}$,

代入消去$\frac{dy}{dx}$得$q(x)=C'(x)e^{-\int p(x)dx}$,

因此$C(x)=\int q(x)e^{\int p(x)dx}dx+C$,

回代得$y=e^{-\int p(x)dx}(\int q(x)e^{\int p(x)dx}dx+C)$.

**5**

考虑方程

$\frac{dy}{dx}+p(x)y=q(x)$,

其中$p(x)$和$q(x)$都是以$\omega>0$为周期的连续函数.试证:

(1)

若$q(x)\equiv0$,则方程(2.42)的任一非零解以$\omega$为周期,当且仅当函数$p(x)$的平均值

$\overline{p}:=\frac{1}{\omega}\int_0^\omega p(x)dx=0$.

(2)

若$q(x)$不恒为零,则方程有唯一的$\omega$周期解,当且仅当$\overline{p}\neq0$.试求出此解.

**解答**

(1)

$q\equiv0$时,这是一阶线性齐次方程,故由通解公式:$
y=Ce^{-\int_0^xp(s)ds}
$.

若$y$以$\omega$为周期,则$y(x+\omega)=y(x)$:

$Ce^{-\int_0^{x+\omega}p(s)ds}=Ce^{-\int_0^xp(s)ds}$

$\iff\int_x^{x+\omega}p(s)ds=0$

$\iff\int_0^\omega p(s)ds=0$

$\iff\frac{1}{\omega}\int_0^\omega p(s)ds=0$.

(2)

$q\not\equiv0$时,这是一阶线性非齐次方程,故由通解公式:

$y=e^{-\int p(x)dx}(C+\int q(x)e^{\int p(x)dx}dx)$.

若$y$以$\omega$为周期,则$y(x+\omega)=y(x)$:

$e^{-\omega\overline{p}}(C+\int_o^{x+\omega}q(t)e^{\int_0^tp(s)ds}dt)=(C+\int_0^xq(t)e^{\int_0^tp(s)ds}dt)$.

若$\overline{p}\neq0$,则$e^{\int_0^\omega p(s)ds}\neq1$.

拆分$\int_0^{x+\omega}=\int_0^\omega+\int_\omega^{x+\omega}$,令$t=\tau+\omega $,则

$\int_\omega^{x+\omega}q(t)e^{\int_0^tp(s)ds}dt=\int_0^xq(\tau+\omega)e^{\int_0^{\tau+\omega}p(s)ds}d\tau$.

$p(t)$周期为$\omega$,则

$\int_0^{\tau+\omega}p(s)ds=\int_0^\omega p(s)ds+\int_0^{\tau+\omega}p(s)ds=\omega\overline{p}+\int_0^\tau p(s)ds$,

由于$q(t)$周期为$\omega$,则

$\int_0^\omega q(\tau+\omega)e^{\omega\overline{p}}e^{\int_0^\tau p(s)ds}d\tau=e^{\omega\overline{p}}\int_0^xq(\tau)e^{\int_0^\tau p(s)ds}d\tau$.

回代得

$e^{-\omega\overline{p}}(C+\int_0^xq(t)e^{\int_0^tp(s)ds}dt+e^{-\omega\overline{p}}\int_0^xq(t)e^{\int_0^tp(s)ds}dt)$

$=C+\int_0^xq(t)e^{\int_0^tp(s)ds}dt$,

解得$C=\frac{1}{e^{\omega\overline{p}}-1}\int_0^\omega q(t)e^{\int_0^tp(s)ds}dt$,

代入通解得周期解:

$y=e^{-\int_0^xp(x)dx}(\frac{1}{e^{w\overline{p}}-1}\int_0^wq(t)e^{\int_0^tp(s)ds}dt+\int_0^xq(t)e^{\int_0^tp(s)ds}dt)$.

**1**

求解下列微分方程:

(2)

$y'=\frac{2y-x+5}{2x-y-4}$;

(4)

$y'=x^3y^3-xy$.

**解答**

(2)

这是齐次方程,令$u=x-1,v=y+2$,则$\frac{dv}{du}=\frac{2v-u}{2u-v}$.

令$w=\frac{v}{u}$,则$w+u\frac{dw}{du}=\frac{2w-1}{2-w}$,

当$w=\pm1$时,$\frac{2-w}{w^2-1}dw=\frac{dx}{u}$,积分得

$\frac{1}{2}\ln|w-1|-\frac{3}{2}\ln|w+1|=\ln|u|+C$,

即$(y+x+3)(x-1)^3=C(x+y+1)^3$,$C\neq0$且$C\neq\infty$;

当$w=1$时,$y=x-3$或$y=-x+1$,恰好对应$C=0$和$C\to\infty$情形,

故通解为$(y-x+3)(x-1)^2=C(x+y+1)^3$.

(4)

这是Bernoulli方程:$\frac{dy}{dx}+\frac{1}{x}y=x^3y^3$.

当$y\ne0$时,

令$z=y^{1-3}=y^{-2}$,则有$\frac{dz}{dx}-2xz=-2x^3$,

这是一阶线性非齐次方程,故由通解公式,

$y=e^{2\int xdx}(C-2\int x^3e^{-2\int xdx}dx)$,

则$y=e^{x^2}(C+\frac{1}{4}e^{-2x^2}(2x^2+1))$,

即$y=Ce^{x^2}+\frac{1}{4}e^{-x^2}(2x^2+1)$.

$y=0$时,$y\equiv0$.

**2**

利用适当的变换,求解下列方程:

(2)$(3uv+v^2)du+(u^2+uv)dv=0$;

(4)$\frac{dy}{dx}=\frac{2x^3+3xy^2-7x}{3x^2y+2y^3-8y}$.

**解答**

(2)

这是齐次方程,令$v=tu$,则

$(3u^3t+t^2u^2)dt+(u^2+u^2t)(tdu+udt)=0$,

$\frac{du}{dt}=-\frac{1+t}{2t(2+t)}dt=-(\frac{1}{4t}+\frac{1}{4(2+t)})dt$.

积分得$\ln|u|=-\frac{1}{4}\ln|t|-\frac{1}{4}\ln|t+2|+C$,

因此$u^4=\frac{C}{t(t+2)}$,即$2u^3v+u^2v^2=C$.

(4)

令$u=x^2$,$v=y^2$,

由$\frac{dy}{dx}=\frac{x(2x^2+3y^2-7)}{y(3x^2+2y^2-8)}$,则有$\frac{dv}{du}=\frac{2u+3v-7}{3u+2v-8}$.

令$s=u-2$,$t=v-1$,则$\frac{dt}{ds}=\frac{2s+3t}{3s+2t}$,

令$t=ws$,则$w+s\frac{dw}{ds}=\frac{2+3w}{3+2w}$,

$\frac{3+2w}{2(1-w^2)}dw=\frac{ds}{s}=\frac{5dw}{4(1-w)}-\frac{dw}{4(1+w)}$,

积分得$-\frac{5}{4}\ln|1-w|-\frac{1}{4}\ln|1+w|=\ln|s|+C$,

回代得$(x^2-y^2-1)^5(x^2+y^2-3)=C(x^2-2)^{10}$.

## 2.5 积分因子法

---

**1**

求解下列微分方程:

(2)$ydx+(2xy-{e}^{-2y})dy=0$;

(4)$ydx-(x^2+y^2+x)dy=0$.

**解答**

(2)

$\frac{dy}{dx}+2x=e^{\frac{x^2}{2}-y}$,这是一阶线性非齐次方程,故由通解公式:

$x=e^{-\int 2dx}(\ln|y|+C)$.

(4)

$\frac{dx}{dy}=\frac{x^2}{y^2}+y+\frac{x}{y}$,

令$u=\frac{x}{y}$,则$u+y\frac{du}{dy}=u^2y+y+u$,

$\frac{du}{u^2}=dy$,即$\arctan (\frac{x}{y})=y+C$.

**2**

证明:方程

$P(x,y)dx+Q(x,y)dy=0$

有形如$\mu=\mu(\varphi(x,y))$的积分因子的充要条件是

$\frac{\frac{\partial P}{\partial y}-\frac{\partial Q}{\partial x}}{Q\frac{\partial \varphi}{\partial x}-P\frac{\partial \varphi}{\partial y}}=f(\varphi(x,y))$,

并写出这个积分因子.然后将结果应用到下述各种情形,得出存在每一种类型积分因子的充要条件:

(1)$\mu=\mu(x\pm y)$;

(2)$\mu=\mu(x^2+y^2)$;

(3)$\mu=\mu(xy)$;

(4)$\mu=\mu\left(\frac{y}{x}\right)$;

(5)$\mu=\mu(x^\alpha y^\beta)$.

**解答**

必要性:设满足$\frac{\partial (\mu P)}{\partial y}=\frac{\partial (\mu Q)}{\partial x}$,

$\text{LHs}=\mu\frac{\partial P}{\partial y}+P\frac{\partial\mu}{\partial y}=\mu\frac{\partial P}{\partial y}+P\mu '(\varphi)\frac{\partial\varphi}{\partial y}$,

$\text{RHs}=\mu\frac{\partial Q}{\partial x}+Q\frac{\partial\mu}{\partial x}=\mu\frac{\partial Q}{\partial x}+Q\mu '(\varphi)\frac{\partial\varphi}{\partial x}$.

令$\text{LHs}=\text{RHs}$,整理得

$\mu (\frac{\partial P}{\partial y}-\frac{\partial Q}{\partial x})=\mu '(\varphi)(Q\frac{\partial\varphi}{\partial x}-P\frac{\partial\varphi}{\partial y})$,

则$f(x,y)=\frac{\mu '(\varphi)}{\mu}=\frac{\frac{\partial P}{\partial y}-\frac{\partial Q}{\partial x}}{Q\frac{\partial\varphi}{\partial x}-P\frac{\partial\varphi}{\partial y}}$.

充分性:令$\mu (\varphi)=e^{\int f(\varphi)d\varphi}$,则代入条件易证$\frac{\partial (\mu P)}{\partial y}=\frac{\partial (\mu Q)}{\partial x}$.

(1)$f(x\pm y)=\frac{\frac{\partial P}{\partial y}-\frac{\partial Q}{\partial x}}{Q\mp P}$;

(2)$f(x^2y^2)=\frac{\frac{\partial P}{\partial y}-\frac{\partial Q}{\partial x}}{2(xQ-yP)}$;

(3)$f(\frac{x}{y})=\frac{\frac{\partial P}{\partial y}-\frac{\partial Q}{\partial x}}{yQ-xP}$;

(4)$f(\frac{y}{x})=\frac{x^2(\frac{\partial P}{\partial y}-\frac{\partial Q}{\partial x})}{-xP+yQ}$;

(5)$f(x^ay^b)=\frac{x^ay^b(\frac{\partial P}{\partial y}-\frac{\partial Q}{\partial x})}{ayQ-bxP}$.

**4**

证明该定理及其逆定理:

若$\mu=\mu(x,y)$是方程$P(x,y)dx+Q(x,y)dy=0$的一个积分因子,使得

$\mu P(x,y)dx+\mu Q(x,y)dy=d\Phi(x,y)$,

则$\mu(x,y)g(\Phi(x,y))$也是该方程的一个积分因子,其中$g(\cdot)$是任一可微的(非零)函数.

**解答**

- 逆定理证明:

由于$\mu $是积分因子,因此$\mu Pdx+\mu Qdy=0$是恰当方程,

即存在原函数$\Phi(x,y)$使得$\frac{\partial\Phi}{\partial x}=\mu P$,$\frac{\partial\Phi}{\partial y}=\mu Q$.

由于$\mu_1$也是积分因子,

则存在原函数$\Psi(x,y)$使得$\frac{\partial\Psi}{\partial x}=\mu_1P$,$\frac{\partial\Psi}{\partial y}=\mu_1Q$.

令$g(x,y)=\frac{\mu_1}{\mu}$,则$\mu_1=g\mu$,

$\frac{\partial\Psi}{\partial x}=g\mu P=g\frac{\partial\Phi}{\partial x}$,$\frac{\partial\Psi}{\partial y}=g\mu Q=g\frac{\partial\Phi}{\partial y}$.

由于$\frac{\partial ^2\Psi}{\partial x\partial y}=\frac{\partial ^2\Psi}{\partial y\partial x}$,则$\frac{\partial}{\partial x}(g\frac{\partial\Phi}{\partial y})=\frac{\partial}{\partial y}(g\frac{\partial\Phi}{\partial x})$,

由于$\frac{\partial ^2\Phi}{\partial x\partial y}=\frac{\partial ^2\Phi}{\partial y\partial x}$,展开并化简得$\frac{\partial g}{\partial y}\frac{\partial\Phi}{\partial x}=\frac{\partial g}{\partial x}\frac{\partial\Phi}{\partial y}$,

这代表着变换$(x,y)\to(g,\Phi)$的Jacobi行列式

$J=\begin{vmatrix}\frac{\partial g}{\partial x}&\frac{\partial g}{\partial y}\\\frac{\partial \Phi}{\partial x}&\frac{\partial \Phi}{\partial y}\end{vmatrix}=\frac{\partial g}{\partial y}\frac{\partial\Phi}{\partial x}-\frac{\partial g}{\partial x}\frac{\partial\Phi}{\partial y}=0$,

映射$(x,y)\to(g,\Phi)$不是满秩的,

因此$g$与$x,y$无关,仅由$\Phi$决定,即$g=g(\Phi)$,$\mu_1=\mu g(\Phi)$.

已知$\mu_1=\mu g(\Phi)$,代入方程:

$\mu_1Pdx+\mu_1Qdy=g(\Phi)(\mu Pdx+\mu Qdy)=g(\Phi)d\Phi=d(\int g(\Phi)d\Phi)$,

即$\mu_1Pdx+\mu_1Qdy$是一个全微分,故$\mu_1=\mu g(\Phi)$.

- 定理证明:

由于$\mu $是积分因子,因此$\mu Pdx+\mu Qdy=0$是恰当方程,

即存在原函数$\Phi(x,y)$使得$\frac{\partial\Phi}{\partial x}=\mu P$,$\frac{\partial\Phi}{\partial y}=\mu Q$.

在恰当方程两侧同乘$g(\Phi)$可得$\frac{\partial\Phi}{\partial x}=\mu\Phi(x,y) P$,$\frac{\partial\Phi}{\partial y}=\mu\Phi(x,y) Q$,

令$\mu_1=\mu\Phi(x,y)$,即$\mu_1$也是积分因子.
