**2**

设随机变量$X$服从自由度为$n$的$\chi^2$分布,证明:

$E(X^k)=\frac{2^k\Gamma(n/2+k)}{\Gamma(n/2)},\quad k=1,2,\dots$.

特别地,有$E(X)=n,D(X)=2n$​.

**解答**

$EX^k=\int_{0}^{+\infty}x^kf(x)dx$

$=\int_{0}^{+\infty}x^k\frac{x^{\frac{n}{2}-1}e^{-\frac{x}{2}}}{2^{\frac{n}{2}}\Gamma(\frac{n}{2})}dx$.

令$t=\frac{x}{2}$,则$dx=2dt$,

$EX^k=\frac{2^k}{\Gamma(\frac{n}{2})}\int_{0}^{+\infty}t^{\frac{n}{2}+k-1}e^{-t}dt=\frac{2^k\Gamma(\frac{n}{2}+k)}{\Gamma(\frac{n}{2})}$,

$EX=\frac{2^1\Gamma(\frac{n}{2}+1)}{\Gamma(\frac{n}{2})}=2\cdot\frac{n}{2}=n$,

$EX^2=\frac{4^1\Gamma(\frac{n}{2}+2)}{\Gamma(\frac{n}{2})}=4(\frac{n}{2}+1)\frac{n}{2}=n^2+2n$,

$DX=EX^2-(EX)^2=2n$.

**6**

设随机变量$X$服从参数为$\alpha,\lambda$的伽马分布$Ga(\alpha,\lambda),\alpha>0,\lambda>0$.证明:

(1)$X$的特征函数为$\varphi(t)=\left(1-\frac{it}{\lambda}\right)^{-\alpha}$;

(2)$E(X^k)=\frac{\Gamma(\alpha+k)}{\Gamma(\alpha)}\frac{1}{\lambda^k}$,特别地,$E(X)=\frac{\alpha}{\lambda},D(X)=\frac{\alpha}{\lambda^2}$;

(3)如果$X_i\sim\text{Ga}(\alpha_i,\lambda),i=1,2,\dots,n$且$X_1,X_2,\dots,X_n$相互独立,则

$\sum_{i=1}^nX_i\sim\text{Ga}(\alpha_1+\alpha_2+\dots+\alpha_n,\lambda);$

(4)若取$\alpha=n/2,\lambda=1/2$,则$\text{Ga}(n/2,1/2)$是$\chi^2(n)$分布;

(5)若取$\alpha=1$,则$\text{Ga}(1,\lambda)$​是指数分布.

**解答**

(1)

已知Gamma分布的密度函数为

$f(x)=\begin{cases}\frac{1}{\Gamma(\alpha)}\lambda^\alpha x^{\alpha-1}e^{-\lambda x},&x>0,\\0,&x\le0,\end{cases}$

则$\varphi(t)=Ee^{itX}=\int_{0}^{+\infty}e^{itx}\frac{1}{\Gamma(\alpha)}\lambda^\alpha x^{\alpha-1}e^{-\lambda x}dx$,

令$u=(\lambda-it)x$,则$x=\frac{u}{\lambda-it}$,

$\varphi(t)=\frac{\lambda^\alpha}{\Gamma(\alpha)(\lambda-it)^\alpha}\int_{0}^{+\infty}u^{\alpha-1}e^{-u}du=\left(1-\frac{it}{\lambda}\right)^{-\alpha}$.

(2)

$EX^k=\int_{0}^{+\infty}x^k\frac{1}{\Gamma(\alpha)}\lambda^\alpha x^{\alpha-1}e^{-\lambda x}dx$,

令$t=\lambda x$,则$dt=\lambda dx$,

$EX^k=\frac{1}{\Gamma(\alpha)\lambda^k}\int_{0}^{+\infty}t^{\alpha+k-1}e^{-t}dt=\frac{\Gamma(\alpha+k)}{\Gamma(\alpha)\lambda^k}$,

$EX=\frac{\Gamma(\alpha+1)}{\lambda\Gamma(\alpha)}=\frac{\alpha}{\lambda}$,

$EX^2=\frac{\Gamma(\alpha+2)}{\lambda^2\Gamma(\alpha)}=\frac{\alpha(\alpha+1)}{\lambda^2}$,

$DX=EX^2-(EX)^2=\frac{\alpha}{\lambda^2}$.

(3)

由于$X_1,X_2,\dots,X_n$独立,

则利用特征函数:

$\varphi_Y(t)=\prod_{i=1}^n\varphi_{X_i}(t)=\left(1-\frac{it}{\lambda}\right)^{-\sum_{i=1}^n\alpha_i}$,

则$\sum_{i=1}^nX_i\sim\text{Ga}\left(\sum_{i=1}^n\alpha_i,\lambda\right)$.

(4)

取$\alpha=\frac{n}{2},\lambda=\frac{1}{2}$,则密度函数变为

$f_1(x)=\begin{cases}\frac{1}{\Gamma(\frac{n}{2})}2^{\frac{n}{2}}x^{\frac{n}{2}-1}e^{-\frac{x}{2}},&x\ge0,\\0,&x<0,\end{cases}$

则$X\sim\chi^2(n)$.

(5)

取$\alpha=1$,则密度函数变为

$f_2(x)=\begin{cases}\lambda e^{-\lambda x},&x>0,\\0,&x\le0,\end{cases}$

则$X\sim E(\lambda)$.

以$f_n(x)$表示自由度为$n$的$t$变量的密度函数,证明:

$$\lim_{n\to\infty}f_n(x)=\frac{1}{\sqrt{2\pi}}e^{-\frac{x^2}{2}}.$$​

**解答**

Stirling公式:$n!\sim\sqrt{2\pi n}\left(\frac{n}{e}\right)^n$,

且$\lim_{n\to+\infty}\Gamma(n)\sim n!$,

则$\lim_{n\to+\infty}f_n(t)\sim\lim_{n\to+\infty}\frac{(\frac{n+1}{2})!}{\sqrt{(\frac{n}{2})!}\sqrt{n\pi}}\left(1+\frac{t^2}{n}\right)^{-\frac{n+1}{2}}$

$\sim\sqrt{\frac{n}{2}}\cdot\frac{1}{\sqrt{n\pi}}e^{-\frac{t^2}{2}}=\frac{1}{\sqrt{2\pi}}e^{-\frac{t^2}{2}}$.
