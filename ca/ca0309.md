**1**

令$f$是复变函数且在$z_0\in\mathbb{C}$处是$ \mathbb{R}$-可微的.证明:当$z\to z_0$时,点集$\{\frac{f(z)-f(z_0)}{z-z_0}\}$的极限点的集合组成了以$ \frac{\partial f}{\partial z}(z_0) $为圆心,半径为$|\frac{\partial f}{\partial \overline{z}}(z_0)|$的圆.

**解答**

令$\Delta z=z-z_0$,$f$在$z_0$处$ \mathbb{R}$-可微,则

$f(z)-f(z_0)=\Delta f(z)=\frac{\partial f}{\partial z}\Delta z+\frac{\partial f}{\partial \overline{z}}\overline{\Delta z}+o(|\Delta z|)$,

$\frac{f(z)-f(z_0)}{\Delta z}\to\frac{\partial f}{\partial z}+\frac{\partial f}{\partial \overline{z}}\cdot\frac{\overline{\Delta z}}{\Delta z},\quad\Delta z\to0$.

令$\Delta z=\rho e^{i\theta}$,则$\overline{\Delta z}=\rho e^{-i\theta}$.

因此

$\frac{f(z)-f(z_0)}{\Delta z}\to\frac{\partial f}{\partial z}+\frac{\partial f}{\partial \overline{z}}\cdot e^{-2i\theta}$.

这正是以$\frac{\partial f}{\partial z}$为圆心,半径为$|\frac{\partial f}{\partial \overline{z}}|$的圆.

**3**

函数$f$在$z_0\in\mathbb{C}$处是$\mathbb{C}$-可微的且$f'(z_0)\neq0$.假设$f$在以$z_0$为圆心,$r>0$为半径的圆盘$B(z_0,r)$上有定义,$ l $为通过$f(z_0)$点的任意一条直线.证明:$B(z_0,r)$的像$f(B(z_0,r))$不可能全部落在$ l $的某一侧上.

**解答**

令$\Delta z=z-z_0$,$f$在$z_0$可微,则

$f(z)-f(z_0)=f'(z_0)\Delta z+o(|\Delta z|)$.

令$\varepsilon>0$,$z_1=z_0+\varepsilon\nu$,$z_2=z_0-\varepsilon\nu$,则

$f(z_1)-f(z_0)=f'(z_0)(\varepsilon\nu)$,

$f(z_2)-f(z_0)=f'(z_0)(-\varepsilon\nu)$.

由于$B(z_0,r)$为圆盘,因此总能找到$\nu$,满足$f(\mathbb{z})$不在$L$上.

而$f(z_1)$与$f(z_2)$关于$f(z_0)$对称,因此必在两侧.

**5**

考虑函数$f=u+iv$.证明:在极坐标$(r,\phi)$表示下,柯西-黎曼方程的形式为

$\frac{\partial u}{\partial r}=\frac{1}{r}\frac{\partial v}{\partial \phi},\quad\frac{\partial v}{\partial r}=-\frac{1}{r}\frac{\partial u}{\partial \phi}$;

而且若$f$在$z_0=r_0e^{i\phi_0}\neq0$处可导,则

$f'(z_0)=e^{-i\phi_0}(\frac{\partial u}{\partial r}(r_0,\phi_0)+i\frac{\partial v}{\partial r}(r_0,\phi_0))$.

**解答**

对于极坐标:$\begin{cases}x=r\cos\varphi\\y=r\sin\varphi\end{cases}$,

$\frac{\partial u}{\partial r}=\frac{\partial u}{\partial x}\cdot\frac{\partial x}{\partial r}+\frac{\partial u}{\partial y}\cdot\frac{\partial y}{\partial r}=\frac{\partial u}{\partial x}\cos\varphi+\frac{\partial u}{\partial y}\sin\varphi$,

$\frac{\partial u}{\partial \varphi}=\frac{\partial u}{\partial x}\cdot\frac{\partial x}{\partial \varphi}+\frac{\partial u}{\partial y}\cdot\frac{\partial y}{\partial \varphi}=-\frac{\partial u}{\partial x}r\sin\varphi+\frac{\partial u}{\partial y}r\cos\varphi$,

$\frac{\partial v}{\partial r}=\frac{\partial v}{\partial x}\cdot\frac{\partial x}{\partial r}+\frac{\partial v}{\partial y}\cdot\frac{\partial y}{\partial r}=\frac{\partial v}{\partial x}\cos\varphi+\frac{\partial v}{\partial y}\sin\varphi$,

$\frac{\partial v}{\partial \varphi}=\frac{\partial v}{\partial x}\cdot\frac{\partial x}{\partial \varphi}+\frac{\partial v}{\partial y}\cdot\frac{\partial y}{\partial \varphi}=-\frac{\partial v}{\partial x}r\sin\varphi+\frac{\partial v}{\partial y}r\cos\varphi$.

故极坐标下的C-R方程易证;

设$z=x+iy$,则函数在$z_0=x_0+iy_0$处可导,

$f'(z_0)=\frac{\partial u}{\partial x}+i\frac{\partial v}{\partial x}$,

则由C-R方程:

$\frac{\partial u}{\partial r}+i\frac{\partial v}{\partial r}=(\frac{\partial u}{\partial x}+i\frac{\partial v}{\partial x})(\cos\varphi+i\sin\varphi)$,

即$f'(z_0)=e^{-i\varphi}(\frac{\partial u}{\partial r}+i\frac{\partial v}{\partial r})$.

**7**

令$a,b,c\in\mathbb{C}$和函数$f:\mathbb{C}\to\mathbb{C}$为$f(z)=az^2+b|z|^2+c\overline{z}^2$.证明:$f$在$z\in\mathbb{C}$处可导当且仅当$bz+2c\overline{z}=0$.

**解答**

$f(z+\Delta z)-f(z)$

$=a(z+\Delta z)^2+b|z+\Delta z|^2+c(\overline{z+\Delta z})^2-az^2-b|z|^2-c(\overline{z})^2$

$=(2az+b\overline{z})\Delta z+(bz+2c\overline{z})\overline{\Delta z}+o(|\Delta z|)$.

因此,$f$在$z\in\mathbb{C}$可导当且仅当$bz+2c\overline{z}=0$.

**9**

寻找$\mathbb{C}$上全纯且$\operatorname{Re}f(z)=e^x(x\cos y-y\sin y)$,$z=x+iy$的函数.

**解答**

设$f(z)=u(x,y)+iv(x,y)$.
已知$u(x,y)=e^x(x\cos y-y\sin y)$.
由于$f$在$\mathbb{C}$上全纯,因此满足C-R方程:

$\frac{\partial u}{\partial x}=\frac{\partial v}{\partial y},\quad\frac{\partial u}{\partial y}=-\frac{\partial v}{\partial x}$.

因此

$v(x,y)=e^x(x\sin y+y\cos y)+C$,

则

$f(z)=e^x(x\cos y-y\sin y)+ie^x(x\sin y+y\cos y)+C
=ze^z+C$.
